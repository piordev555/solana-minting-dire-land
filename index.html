THE STORY

About Us

We are a team dedicated to educating Ethiopian, Eritrean, and African people on Web 3.

We believe this technology can be a tool for people to discover new ways and ideas to run a business, sell art, or fund projects. One of the things we want to build through this project is an NFT marketplace or platform specifically designed for this community.

Dire Lej is created by an Ethiopian diaspora who wants to give back to her homeland by putting NFTs, crypto, and the metaverse to good use.
